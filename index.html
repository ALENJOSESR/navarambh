About Us

Welcome to Navarambh International Preschool

Children are natural learners and allowing them to explore is the pathway to healthy learning. At Navarambh, we celebrate every child and their uniqueness. An age - appropriate curriculum and an unhurried childhood are the most important qualities of ours. Children learn through imitation and want to explore and experience themselves.

We wish to collaborate a healthy environment through different methodologies from Rudolf Steiner’s rhythm, repetition and reverence, Maria Montessori’s self - directed learning and Jean Piaget’s play as an integral part of development. The facilitators are loving, active, responsible and passionate about their work.

Curriculum

Our Curriculum

The curriculum is carefully designed to meet the overall development of the child. It focuses on the physical, cognitive and logical thinking, fine and gross motor skills, sensorial development, social - emotional, eye - hand coordination, language work through stories, rhymes and songs, vocabulary building, alphabets with sounds, Fun with numbers, exploration, creativity and encouraging curiosity in every child.

Mission

Mission

We strive to cherish a free young primary schooler with a memorable childhood, fun filled, independent, curious and be able to adapt to new environments.

Vision

Vision

To create life - long learners through an unhurried childhood that gives them opportunities to explore, engage and enlighten

Play Group
Our toddler group is an age group from 1.5 years - 2.5 years. Children in the toddler group need utmost care and support from the adults. Our facilitators and assistant make sure of the safety and well - being of the child.
Nursery
The Nursery group is between 2.5 years - 3.5 years. Children learn through imitation and the curriculum focuses on hands - on activities.
Junior Kindergarten
The Junior Kindergarten is between 3.5 years - 4.5 years. The hands - on activities to learn literacy and numeracy at this stage. Children learn through repetition and rhythm.
Senior Kindergarten
The senior kindergarten is between 4.5 yrs-5.5 yrs. The curriculum focuses on the overall development of the child in literacy, numeracy and a second language in an experiential learning environment
Day Care
The Day Care is between 1.5 years - 10 years. The child is fed with the healthy meal provided by the parent, nap time and play time. For older students, the homework are taken care of as per the Parent request.
